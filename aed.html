AED & Assisted CPR

RISD MIT Product Design & Development, Spring 2019


Executive Summary

The HeartJoules team is developing an improved cardiac arrest treatment device, with the goal of increasing survival rates for cardiac arrest patients. To date, our team has evaluated potential market segments, conducted over 20 interviews, generated over 25 concepts, and created sketch models. We are currently pursuing two potential concepts; 1, an IoT-enabled AED, to allow for faster location of patients by first responders; 2, a device that will assist bystanders in performing manual chest compressions on patients. In the coming weeks, we expect to device whether to pursue one or both of these options. More detailed findings from our customer studies can be found in this progress report.

Mission Statement

To save the lives of more cardiac arrest patients by making treatment devices more accessible and easier to use.
