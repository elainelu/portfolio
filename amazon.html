Amazon Alexa

UX Design Internship, Summer 2018


I worked with the Alexa communications design team in the Devices and Services Design Group (DDG).

The Alexa communications domain inclues: calling, messaging, drop in, contacts and announcements. Over 12 weeks, my project was to design an experience for communications to exist within the exiting framework of screened Echo devices. In my last week, I presented six rounds of the final design concept to PMs, developers, design directors and our VP of Design to continue the project’s lifecycle. The feature is currently scheduled to ship next year.




Brief: Create a delightful home screen experience to raise awareness and increase engagement for comms.

The home screen for Echo devices is a place that presents light weight, glanceable information that may be relevant to the customer. Currently, the comms team only has a single “presence” card on screened Echo devices that is tightly integrated with Drop in. I framed a series of concepts that will drive engagement for communications and create moments of delight from these home panels.
